About NutriSync

Science-backed meal planning that adapts to your body, goals, and lifestyle.

Our Mission

At NutriSync, we believe nutrition should be personal—not generic. While most meal plans ignore critical factors like age, gender, or health conditions, our algorithm creates truly customized weekly plans using real-time nutritional data.

How It Works

1. Your Profile

Enter your age, weight, gender, and health goal (weight loss, muscle gain, diabetes control).

2. Smart Calculation

Our system calculates your daily caloric needs and queries the Spoonacular API for recipes matching your macros.

3. Your Plan

Receive a complete weekly meal prep plan with shopping list, nutritional breakdown, and cooking instructions.

Data Privacy

Your health data is never stored on our servers. All calculations happen in your browser, and API requests contain only anonymized nutritional parameters—not personal identifiers. We use the Spoonacular API for ingredient and recipe data.
